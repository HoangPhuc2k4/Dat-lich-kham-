About the Specialist

Dr. Julian St. Clair is a board-certified cardiologist whose approach merges advanced surgical interventions with a deep commitment to patient lifestyle integration. Educated at Johns Hopkins and having completed his residency at Mayo Clinic, he focuses on the "Whole-Heart" philosophy.

His practice at The Curated Clinic emphasizes early detection and personalized health blueprints. He has been recognized as "Physician of the Year" three times and has published over 40 research papers on non-invasive cardiac monitoring.
